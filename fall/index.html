Thanks for your interest in our study! 

We are a research team in Human Centered Design and Engineering at the University of Washington. We are conducting this study to evaluate the web page design for social events. You will read an advertisement of the social events and get asked about how you would evaluate the event.

Once you complete the study, you will get paid $0.7 regardless of whether you positively or negatively evaluate the social event. However, we will ask you questions about the content to check whether you understand it or not, so please take your time to read the information about the social event. Your answer is really important to our research. It will take about 7 minutes. 

Your participation is voluntary, and you are free to stop your participation at any moment. If you are 18 years of age or older, you understood the statement above, and freely consent to participate in the study, please click the “Next” to start the study.
