Once you complete the survey, you will get paid $0.6 regardless of your intention to take the course. Please honestly answer the questions based on your current future plans.

Your participation is voluntary, and you are free to stop your participation at any moment. The whole study will take less than 5 minutes. If you are 18 years of age or older, and consent to participate in the study, please click the “Next”
