YOGA Practice with Nicky Benfield


WILL YOU JOIN?

Yes No

DESCRIPTION

We are offering a free live-streamed yoga session with Nicky Benfield.You can learn relaxation techniques, work on your alignment, and build strength and flexibility. No matter where you live, you can practice yoga with Nicky using a computer or smartphone. No prior yoga experience required.


You will enhance your mood, energy, and even flexibility after practicing with Nicky just for 40-minutes! It would be also convenient and comfortable to practice yoga in your own room. Nicky's instruction is easy to follow. You will feel relaxed afterward and sleep well after a doing a day of yoga practice.

ABOUT THE EVENT HOST

Nicky Benfield is a Yoga lover and teacher who has over 2,000 hours of experience as a registered teacher with the Yoga Alliance previously in Denver, Colorado and now in Seattle, Washington.She wants to make yoga acessible to all so has started to live-stream her yoga class online
