Hike Rattle Snake Ledge Saturday Morning

  • Tell a friend
  • Share

  • Saturday, April 9, 2016

    10:00 AM

  • Rattlesnake Ledge Parking Lot

    18618 Cedar falls Rd. SE, North Bend, WA (map)

    47.485275 -121.770103

  • Now that the weather's getting better let's do a hike to Rattle Snake Ledge. Rattle Snake Ledge is one of the most popular trails nearby Seattle. It is a 4 mile out and back trail that offers scenic views and is good for all skill levels. The view from the top is well worth the hike up. From the top, you can see mountain and lake views. On a sunny day, the lake looks positively blue. There is also a nice lake at the beginning of trail where you can swim, float, or even picnic at the park. Let's go and check it out! I promise you will not be disappointed.


    We'll meet at the parking lot next to the trail entrance at 10:00 am. I can do carpool from Seattle since I have early morning errands to run at UW. If you need carpool, let's meet at the Burke Museum parking lot at 9:00 am. Call/text me at 206-933-0036 if you have trouble finding me.
