UW Students: Free Drop-in Yoga & Meditation Class

Find your inner peace and strength!

  • WHY?
    This class is for beginners and experienced alike. Each class session will feature exercises to develop breath awareness, meditation (guided), balance, alignment (correct posture), and flexibility. This class is appropriate for all levels and all body types - adaptations to make each pose just right for each student will be offered. Be healthy and relaxed!

    Jenine became a Registered Yoga Teacher (200 Hours) through Yoga Alliance in 2015 and is a practitioner of Soto Zen meditation. Her approach is to teach with humor and humility, finding ways to see physical challenges as opportunities rather than limitations. In Jenine’s class, you’ll discover a new awareness of your breath, body, and heart. Combining exercises and techniques for stress reduction and relaxation, you’ll learn to stretch and relax in a way that works for you. Jenine teaches Gentle Yoga & Meditation at the IMA.


    HOW?
    IMA is easily accessible via a bus or Link light rail! Studio 316 is one floor above the main entrance to the IMA at the end of the hall. To enter IMA, you will need a valid UW ID. For those people who do not have a yoga mat, the studio has mats and props available free of charge.

    This is a group for anyone who has encountered the Seattle Freeze and persisted in the face of it -- whether you're new in town and wondering why it's so hard to connect, a long-term transplant who's all too familiar with the chill, or a native who wants to break out of the Seattle shell, we're here to give you a group to hang out with and some like-minded people who are also looking for friends, not just being "Seattle nice".

    Here's a great Seattle Times article about the Freeze: http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/pacificnw/2005/0213/cove....

    Come join the group, and do your part towards the Thaw!
