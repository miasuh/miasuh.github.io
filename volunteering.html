Volunteer at Bacon, Eggs & Kegs (Sunday)

  • Export
    • Outlook
    • iCal
    • Google
    • Yahoo!
  • Tell a friend
  • Share

  • Sunday, March 13, 2016

    9:30 AM     to 3:30 PM

  • Century Link Field

    800 Occidental Ave S, Seattle, WA (map)

    47.594486 -122.331497

  • Join us for the most delicious beer fest of them all! We are making Bacon, Eggs & Kegs happen on Sunday, March 13th and we need awesome people like you to make it happen. Volunteers at this event will be assisting with beer pouring, the TWIF Bloody Mary Bars, and other general event tasks. We have two shifts to choose from and each one comes with its own special perks of being a volunteer.

    What is Bacon, Eggs & Kegs you ask? Well, its exactly as it sounds a delicious food inspired beer festival where you can try Seattles best bacon and egg inspired foods paired tons of options for ice cold beer. Not a beer drinker? No worries, weve got the best Bloody Marys this side of the Mississippi waiting for you. What makes this even more delicious? The proceeds from this event give back to the Seattle community through support of The World is Funs programs!

    Volunteers will be scheduled from 9:30am to 3:30pm which includes a pizza lunch. Upon completion of your shift, you and your fellow volunteers will be invited to a special volunteer appreciation party that starts immediately after your shift. This is basically our very own private beer fest just for you and your new volunteer friends.

    Shift time: SUNDAY 9:30am to 3:30pm
